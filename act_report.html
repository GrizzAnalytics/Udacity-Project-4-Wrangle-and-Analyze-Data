plt.ylabel('Favorites', size = 15)
plt.xlabel('Retweets', size = 15)
plt.savefig('rvf.jpg')

Analysis:¶

It seems like there is a correlation between the amount of likes/favorites a tweet will get and the amount of times that tweet will get retweeted. It seems like more retweets a tweet will get, the more favorites it will get as well. Or at least, thats what the data shows. I'm not entirely it works all that in real life twitter sadly.
